### Hi All! Very happy to be a vendor on Rennlist! I started out with my first Porsche (914) almost 40 years ago and have a passion for all things Porsche. Please reach out with any questions and I'm looking forward to meeting new friends and helping capture some epic memories. Below, please find the details of our RS5 for all 3rd gen Cayenne models. RoadSpy SUPERMOUNTS are custom-fit for your exact vehicle’s rear view mirror base. SUPERMOUNTS offer the most clutter-free & secure mount for your action camera, cellphone or radar detector by using GoPro-style adapters. GoPro cameras will fit right up to the RS5 and a 1/4" adapter is included for other camera types. Mounted up high and on the vehicle’s centerline, your camera will capture the best possible viewing angle of the road ahead. Almost invisible to onlookers without a device attached. You may never use a suction cup mount again. A cellphone mount is also included. What's in the box: RS5 Mount, Thumbscrew, Thumbscrew tightener, 1/4" camera adapter, phone mount, storage pouch, instruction QRC.
The RS5 is designed to fit:
  * 2020-2025 (992) 911
  * 2024-2025 Macan
  * 2020-2025 Taycan
  * 2017-2025 Panamera
  * 2019-2025 Cayenne


  * Conforms to interior rear view mirror base. No permanent modification to your Porsche. 
  * Rock-Solid mount designed to fit GoPro cameras, other cameras, cellphones or radar detectors. 
  * Mounted up high and on car's centerline, your camera will capture the best possible view. 
  * Designed in Florida, USA. Manufactured from quality automotive grade materials. 
  * Super easy 2-minute installation.
  * Easily removed. Quick attach/detach of your camera. 
  * NO SUCTION CUP FAILS! The Only Mount You Need.
