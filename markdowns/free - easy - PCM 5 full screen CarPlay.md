![Default](https://rennlist.com/forums/images/icons/icon1.gif) **free - easy - PCM 5 full screen CarPlay**
A little background: I'm a software engineer who bought a Macan with PCM 5 a couple of months ago. I was annoyed seeing all of the vendors taking advantage of the community and charging ridiculous amounts for CarPlay mods. So, I did some reverse engineering on my Macan, made this open-source mod kit for the community and made some CarPlay mods for it. Recently, other model owners (992, Cayenne) have tested the CarPlay mod to be working so I am trying to share it with those communities! (if someone with a Panamera or 718 can confirm it works - that would be great too!) Link to instructions and downloads: [MH2p SD ModKit](https://rennlist.com/forums/cayenne-9y0-2019/<https:/lawpaul.github.io/MH2p_SD_ModKit_Site/>) All you need is a computer and an SD card or flash drive that can plug into your vehicle! This is applicable for Cayennes from 2019-2022(?). After that they have PCM 6 which already includes full screen CarPlay! What this does (sorry - don't have pics from a Cayenne yet, just 992 and Macan): changes OEM CarPlay: ![](https://cimg1.ibsrv.net/gimg/rennlist.com-vbulletin/1024x682/992_orig_25d139f63517ccdab7ca198e7474b8e20159dce0.jpg) to either "windowed" full screen: ![](https://cimg3.ibsrv.net/gimg/rennlist.com-vbulletin/1024x768/992_windowed_11a0506c522bd9b8d9da1321c15549fc11bfd803.jpg) or "true" full screen: ![](https://cimg8.ibsrv.net/gimg/rennlist.com-vbulletin/1024x768/992_true_7589a8f4566ea491983c254a457dd60d79b6da7f.jpg) Let me know if you have any questions!
